Q3 Financial Report - Acme Corporation

Executive Summary:
Acme Corporation maintains a strong liquidity position with an estimated operational runway of 18 months under current burn rates of $2.1M per month. Revenue increased 12% quarter-over-quarter, reaching $8.4M. Operating expenses remained stable at $6.3M.

Key Highlights:
- Cash reserves: $37.8M as of September 30, 2025
- Revenue growth: 12% QoQ, driven by enterprise segment
- Customer acquisition cost reduced by 8%
- Net retention rate: 115%
- Headcount: 142 employees across 3 offices

Risk Factors:
- Delayed receivables from Q2 contracts totaling $4.2M
- Currency exposure in APAC markets
- Increased competition from TechRival Inc. in the mid-market segment

Outlook:
Management expects continued growth momentum into Q4, with new product launches expected to drive 15-20% revenue increase. Capital expenditure planned at $1.2M for infrastructure upgrades.
